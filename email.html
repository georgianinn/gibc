GIBC Has a New Owner Management System!

It's time to get your new account set up.

All GIBC Owners are recieving this invitation today. GIBC has partnered with VitalGuest/GuestCentric to provide some of the following features:

  • Ownership management
  • Online due payment
  • Online reservations
  • A streamlined check in / check out experience
  • Storage of payment methods for future payments
  • Automated rental dispursements and account credits

More features are coming soon, including:

  • Guest of owner reservations
  • Bonus week management
  • Owner rate booking engine
  • Auotmated owner functions

We're excited to bring these new services to our owners, and are looking forward to answering any questions or comments you may have.

Once your new account is set up, take a look around our new website! You can always access the new owner's portal from the "Owners" page of the website, where there is a log in link. Make sure to save your username and password somewhere so you can access your account! If you forget your password, please navigate to the log-in page and click the button for "Forgot Password" and we will automatically email you a new one. Thanks for being an Owner at GIBC, and we hope you like these upgrades!

To set up your account, please click on the button below. You will enter some basic information to confirm your owner data and then can log in and get familiar with the new portal. If you have any questions, please call or email us at the front desk!
